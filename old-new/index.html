Hello, I am a human who has created some things—

I make music in various forms, am slowly learning about photography, and occasionally will experiment with things like painting and animation. Here you'll find a selection of various thoughts and creations of mine.

Portfolio


Music
Since April 2020 I've been releasing what I've been calling a "Notebook Album" each month, full of random musical experiments. Between this and other projects, I've been writing on piano, a tiny bit of cello, and I've written a number of scores for instruments I have no clue how to play, which are mainly in here as MIDI playback using MuseScore.

Photography
Over the last few months I've been taking and editing some photos, experimenting with a tiny bit of film photography, and learning more about visual composition and the like. Here are some of the more interesting things I've captured.
